about me

I am a UX / UI designer based in Madrid.

I am passionate about creating intuitive and practical designs aimed at improving the user experience.

I have developed a passion for technology, always Iooking to expand my tech knowledge, therefore, after working as a Customer Service Specialist at Arbor Fintech, I decided to immerse myself in the world of design through the UX / UI Design bootcamp at Ironhack.

I consider myself an autodidact, entrepreneur, passionate about technology and very creative person.

Currently, I am designing user experiences in 2gether, a cryptocurrency platform. At the same time, I am designing intuitive user interfaces as a freelancer for Dudyfit, a fitness startup.

About my entrepreneurship experience, I have been involved for 4 years in the startup environment. At the age of 17, I won the second prize in "La Caixa Bank" entrepreneurship contest and, at the age of 21, I was part of the Santander Bank's Yuzz program in Deusto Business School. Also, I became the main organizer of events for StartUC3M, the entrepreneurship association of Carlos 3rd University.
