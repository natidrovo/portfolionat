about me

I am a UX / UI designer based in Madrid.

I am passionate about creating intuitive and practical designs aimed at improving the user experience.

Currently, I am working in 2gether, a cryptocurrency platform, as a UX designer focused on analyzing users' behaviour and userflows within the app, mapping the pain points and seeking for solutions in order to provide users with an enhanced experience.

At the same time, I am working as freelancer in view of learning about how to design diverse digital products for different kind of users.

As a freelancer, the biggest projects I have worked in were a project for Dudydfit, a fitness startup, and another one for BeoBeogo, a healthy food delivery platform.
